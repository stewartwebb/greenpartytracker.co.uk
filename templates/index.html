Green Party Membership Over Time: A Complete History

The Green Party of England and Wales has experienced remarkable membership growth since 2023. This tracker provides a comprehensive, regularly-updated record of Green Party membership numbers over time, allowing supporters, journalists, and political analysts to monitor the trajectory of one of the UK's fastest-growing political movements.

How Many Green Party Members Are There?

As of March 2026, the Green Party has over 216,000 members, making it one of the largest political parties in the United Kingdom by membership. This represents a staggering growth of over 300% from the approximately 53,000 members recorded in December 2023. The Green Party membership tracker above shows this growth in detail, with data points captured from official announcements and verified sources.

Green Party Membership Growth Timeline

The Green Party membership history reveals several key phases of growth:

  • December 2023 – June 2024: Steady baseline of around 53,000–55,000 members, reflecting the party's established support base.
  • March 2025: Membership begins climbing to 60,000, signalling the start of an acceleration driven by increased public awareness of climate issues and disillusionment with mainstream parties.
  • September – October 2025: An extraordinary surge takes membership from 68,500 to 140,000 in just eight weeks — a period of explosive growth unlike anything seen in UK politics.
  • Late 2025 – Early 2026: Continued strong growth through 180,000 (December 2025) to pass the historic 200,000 milestone in March 2026.

Green Party vs Reform UK Membership Comparison

While Reform UK currently leads in raw membership numbers with over 275,000 members, the Green Party's rate of growth has been remarkable. The gap between the two parties has been narrowing steadily, and at current trends, the Green Party could overtake Reform UK in total membership. This tracker allows you to compare both parties' membership trajectories side by side.

Why Is Green Party Membership Growing?

Several factors have contributed to the surge in Green Party membership numbers:

  • Climate urgency: Growing public concern about climate change and extreme weather events has driven voters toward the party with the strongest environmental credentials.
  • Electoral success: The Green Party's breakthrough in winning four MPs at the 2024 general election demonstrated electoral viability and attracted new supporters.
  • Policy platform: The party's comprehensive manifesto addressing wealth inequality, public services, and the green economic transition has resonated with a broad coalition of voters.
  • Community organising: Grassroots campaigns and local election wins have built momentum in communities across England and Wales.

About This Tracker

The Green Party Membership Tracker is an independent project that collates publicly available membership data to provide a transparent, accessible record of Green Party membership over time. Data is sourced from official party announcements, press releases, and verified reports. Reform UK membership data is tracked from their official website, and Restore Britain data from public announcements.

This tracker is updated regularly to ensure accuracy. If you have verified membership data that should be included, please get in touch.
